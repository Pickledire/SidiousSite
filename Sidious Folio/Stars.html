The Eternal Empire

"The dark side of the Force is a pathway to many abilities some consider to be unnatural."

For a thousand years, the Sith waited in the shadows. Patient. Calculating. The Rule of Two ensured that each generation grew stronger, culminating in the ultimate Sith Lord—Darth Sidious.

Through manipulation and political genius, he dismantled the Republic from within. The Clone Wars were his masterwork, a conflict where he controlled both sides. When the time came, Order 66 extinguished the Jedi, and the Empire rose.

Even death could not contain him. Through the dark side, Sidious prepared for his return on Exegol, building the Final Order to reclaim what was always his.

"Everything that has transpired has done so according to my design."
